Getting a new Hobby

Last year, I learned how to ride a bike, and it became an experience I will not forget. At first, it was really hard for me to balance. Still, I pushed myself to learn because I needed to lose weight.

On my third day of learning how to bike, I was still barely able to balance properly. Even so, my friends made me ride a bike all the way to MOA, which was honestly scary. While riding, I almost got crushed by a truck twice because I was still struggling with control and balance. I also crashed into something near a canal on the way home. Those moments were scary and made me want to quit, but they also helped me learn faster.

Overall, my experience with biking was good despite all the struggles. It taught me a lot of things and not to give up easily. In the end, learning how to ride a bike was worth it.
